1. **Try Multiple AI Tools**

   - Mix AI-generated with designer-made
   - Iterate until perfect

3. **Use Your Color Palette**

   - Apply exact hex codes from the app
   - Maintain brand consistency
   - Test on light/dark backgrounds

4. **Get Feedback**
   - Show to your target audience
   - A/B test different versions
   - Refine based on reactions
